udaclty

Audacity is a for-profit educational organization founded by Sebastian Thrun, David Stavins, and Mike Sokolski. This is to provide Massive Open Online Courses (MOOCs). According to Thran, the origin of the name “Udacity” comes from the company’s desire to be “bold for you, the student.”

udemy

Udemy is an online educational platform for adults, unlike other Mock platforms that offer traditional social programs. The Udemy platform is concerned with many contents, including programming, translation, drawing, making money from the Internet, or creativity in general.

coursera

Coursera is a for-profit online education company that provides massive open courses created by teachers from Stanford University. Coursera works with international universities to offer their educational courses through the Coursera interface for managing educational systems. The number of students enrolled in its courses in October 2013 reached 5 million students from all over the world.
