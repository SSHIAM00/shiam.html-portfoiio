Harry Potter is a fictional character in a series of seven books by British author J. K. Rowling

Which tells the story of the magical boy Harry Potter, from the time he discovered the truth that he was a wizard, until he reached the age of seventeen,

She discovers his past, his magical relationships, and his quest to eliminate the Dark Lord, Lord Voldemort. The book series is accompanied by a series of eight films bearing the same titles as the books.
